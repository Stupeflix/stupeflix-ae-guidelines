Stupeflix After Effects Guidelines
==================================

Stupeflix provides a simple After Effects to Stupeflix Widget workflow. This documentation will guide you through our engine limitations and the supported After Effects features.

This documentation is our little living thing, it regularly evoles and we feed it as regularly as we can. If you have any questions regarding the AE docs, feel free to drop me an email at seb (at) stupeflix [dot] com.
